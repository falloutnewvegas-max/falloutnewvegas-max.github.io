Fallout New Vegas is a 2010 action role-playing video game that was developed by Obsidian Entertainment and published by Bethesda Softworks. The game, which was released for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 3, and Xbox 360, is set in the Mojave Desert of North America 204 years after a devastating nuclear war. The player controls a courier who wakes up after being shot in the head by an unknown assailant. While tracking the assailant, the courier becomes embroiled in a larger conflict between different governing factions that are vying for control of the region.
